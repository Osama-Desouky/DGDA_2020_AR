About DGDA

Vision

To anchor our vision for the future on a jewel from the Saudi past.

Mission

Cultivating Diriyah into a global gathering place by creating rich experiences that narrate the stories of our history, making Diriyah a must-see destination and the pride of all Saudis.

Values

We are
proud

We are proud of who we are and we have to share. For us, our values lead us to excellence; it’s what we strive for, it’s who we are.

Rooted in our
Heritage

We respect our traditions and heritage and are passionate about allowing our people to share this great heritage with the world and build a stronger global community.

Shared
Connection

We value the importance of human connections and consistently strive to build lasting bonds with our local and global communities.

Inclusiveness

We engage and involve the members of our community as they are our family.

Community
Devotion

We offer genuine support to our family, our community and individuals for development and transformation through empowerment.

Authenticity

We are authentic in our spirit. We speak the truth.

Our Mandate

In order to preserve, celebrate and develop the iconic landmark of Diriyah into a top global attraction and the pride of all Saudis, DGDA was established to achieve the following

Globally
Renowned Landmark

To preserve the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Al-Turaif and the natural geological gem of Wadi Hanifah, and revive them as must-see, exceptional attractions for local and international tourists.

Globally
Gathering Place

To develop the Diriyah Gate area and the surrounding Wadi Safar and Al-Bujairi into world-class hospitality, cultural, recreational and retail destinations and transform them into a vibrant, global gathering place.

Empowered
Community

To reinforce our social responsibility towards the Diriyah local community by enriching its people, developing its services and facilities and turning it into a lively, enabled community essentially contributing to a flourishing nation.
